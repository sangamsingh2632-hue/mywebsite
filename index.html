How to Play

  • One tap / button to jump, swing and influence direction.
  • Time your release for shots — hold to build power (if your game supports it).
  • Embrace the chaos: physics flips and surprise modifiers make every match different.

Tips

  • Focus on positioning before going for risky shots.
  • Try to block early by reading the ball’s path.
  • Adapt quickly — no two rounds play the same.
